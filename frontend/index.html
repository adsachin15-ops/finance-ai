Welcome to FinanceAI

Your privacy-first personal finance analyzer

1

Create an Account

Go to Accounts → + New Account and add your bank account (e.g. "HDFC Savings", "ICICI Credit Card").

💡 You can add multiple accounts — savings, wallets, UPI, and cash.
2

Upload Bank Statement

Go to Upload, select your account, and drag & drop your bank statement file.

PDF
⚠️ Important: Files must be without password protection. If your bank statement PDF is locked, remove the password first using any free PDF unlocker.
📄 Download your statement as PDF from your bank's net-banking portal.
3

View Dashboard & Analytics

Once uploaded, head to Overview to see your financial summary — income, expenses, savings rate, and health score.

📊 Spending by Category 📈 Income vs Expense Trends 💰 Net Savings ❤️ Financial Health Score
4

Get AI Insights

Visit Insights for AI-generated tips about your spending patterns, unusual transactions, and savings opportunities.

🔒 All your data stays 100% local — nothing is sent to any cloud or third-party.
ℹ️ Guest Mode: Your data is stored in memory only and will be deleted when you logout or close the browser.
